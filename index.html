Our Ultrasound Services

All Types of Ultrasound Scans

We specialise exclusively in ultrasound — from routine abdominal scans to detailed obstetric imaging and Doppler studies, all under one expert roof.

Obstetric Ultrasound

Complete pregnancy monitoring from early confirmation to growth scans, anomaly scans (Level II), and Doppler studies for mother and baby.

Abdominal Ultrasound

Detailed imaging of liver, gallbladder, kidneys, pancreas, spleen, and intestines to detect stones, cysts, fatty changes, and masses.

Gynaecological Ultrasound

Transabdominal and transvaginal scans for uterus, ovaries, and pelvis — for PCOD, fibroids, follicle monitoring, and more.

Doppler Ultrasound

Colour Doppler and spectral Doppler studies for blood flow analysis in vessels, arteries, veins, and foetal circulation.

Thyroid & Neck Ultrasound

High-frequency imaging of the thyroid gland, lymph nodes, salivary glands, and neck structures for accurate nodule assessment.

Musculoskeletal Ultrasound

Dynamic imaging of muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints for sports injuries, rotator cuff tears, and soft tissue assessment.

About Us

Dedicated Exclusively to Ultrasound

Sonu Scan Centre is a specialised ultrasound-only diagnostic centre established in 2005. By focusing solely on ultrasound, our team has developed unmatched expertise in sonography — giving you more accurate, detailed, and reliable results than any general diagnostic centre.

Our senior sonologist personally performs and interprets every scan, ensuring the highest standard of reporting for your doctor and peace of mind for you.
